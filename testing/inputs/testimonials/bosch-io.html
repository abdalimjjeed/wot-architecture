Bosch.IO GmbH

Bosch.IO is convinced that I(o)T cannot be done alone and that collaboration via Open Source software and open standards are key to provide interoperability in the heterogenous IoT landscape.

We reached the conclusion that WoT Thing Models are a very capable modeling approach to describe capabilities of thing/device classes, having the benefit of relying on JSON-LD for providing additional semantic context. What we were seeking for, modeling thing/device classes, is even taken one step further by being able to introspect a thing's capabilities on the instance level via its WoT Thing Description, including its API endpoints.

By adopting the latest W3C Working Draft 11 March 2022 of WoT Thing Description 1.1 in Eclipse Ditto, an Open Source Digital Twin framework initiated by Bosch.IO and still under very active development, Ditto can link to Thing Models and automatically create and provide Thing Descriptions for its managed digital twins.
